Know the wait before you walk in.

PickupRadar helps UK delivery drivers see and report pickup wait times at restaurants and shops. It is built to reduce wasted time and help drivers make better pickup decisions.

Independent driver tool

PickupRadar is independent and is not connected to Uber Eats, Deliveroo, Just Eat, or any delivery platform. The app does not connect to or interact with your delivery platform accounts.

PickupRadar does not ask for delivery app login details, passwords, account details, customer details, or order information. It only lets drivers share simple pickup wait-time reports.

Please do not share customer names, customer addresses, order numbers, or private order details in reports.
